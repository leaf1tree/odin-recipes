Stir Fried Garlic Sauce Broccoli

A simple, yet satsifying Chinese-style stir fry.

Best paired with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Garlic
  • Corn starch
  • Chicken boullion powder
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Neutral oil

Directions

  1. Mix the sauce first. In a small bowl, add some water, salt, sugar, chicken stock powder, and corn starch. It should be a very thin, slightly viscous solution. This will help the garlic adhere to the broccoli and act as a thin glaze.
  2. To boiling water, blanch broccoli for 30 seconds and drain well.
  3. To a hot pan, heat some oil. Once shimmering, add your broccoli. Cook for 1-2 minutes or until slightly crunchy.
  4. Add garlic and fry until aromatic, another 1 minute.
  5. Add sauce and mix for 30 seconds. Plate and serve immediately.
